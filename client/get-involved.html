Our Mission

At SBC Educational & Welfare Services, our mission is to provide accessible and high-quality nursery education to children in underserved and rural communities. We are committed to nurturing each child's early development in a safe, joyful, and stimulating learning environment that encourages curiosity, creativity, and confidence.

Beyond education, we focus on empowering individuals, especially women, through training, certification, and employment in early childhood education.

We aim to:

  • Lay the foundation for lifelong learning
  • Create dignified livelihoods
  • Promote gender equity
  • Build self-reliant and sustainable societies

We believe in education-led transformation for a more equitable world.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every child, regardless of their background, has the opportunity to begin life with strong educational support through quality nursery programs. We strive for a world where early childhood education is a right, reaching even the most remote communities.

Our dream is to build inclusive communities where:

  • Educators, especially women, are empowered to lead meaningful change
  • Children develop critical thinking skills and emotional intelligence from an early age
  • Families are strengthened through educational support and community engagement
  • Local communities become centers of learning and growth
  • Educational opportunities create pathways out of poverty
  • Cultural diversity is celebrated while building bridges across differences

Through our work, we seek to create a ripple effect where quality early childhood education transforms not just individual lives, but entire communities, fostering resilience, innovation, and hope for generations to come.

SBC Educational & Welfare Services is dedicated to transforming lives by providing world-class education to children from India’s most disadvantaged communities.
